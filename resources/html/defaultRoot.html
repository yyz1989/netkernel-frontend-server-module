Introduction

This is a test platform to publish LOD datasets of the Flemish Government.

Published Datasets

For the moment you will only find the datasets from http://data.opendataforum.info published here according to the DCAT AP profile.

Supporting data are: the classification themes according to the VRIND, related governmental departments and the licenses used.
